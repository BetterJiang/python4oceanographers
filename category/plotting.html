AVISO Altimetry is available in both gridded and track data. Sometime track data is desirable to augment hydrographic data due to its higher resolution and/or to avoid the several assumptions done for the grid interpolation. If order to find the closest track near to your area of interest one must check the satellites available. The AVISO group have a nice kmz compilation with all the satellites tracks availability in time:

http://www.aviso.oceanobs.com/en/data/tools/pass-locator.html

The kmz is just a compressed kml. So it is straight forward to unzip it and read its information with a simple python script. we will need to import zipfile and a kml parser
